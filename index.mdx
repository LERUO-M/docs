Universel is an on/off ramp service (powered by Kotani Pay) that lets users seamlessly buy and sell **uZAR** and **USDC** across supported networks. Whether you're moving between fiat and stablecoins or bridging value across chains, Universel provides a fast, reliable gateway between traditional finance and digital assets.

## What you can do with Universel

- **Purchase/Sell uZAR** — the South African Rand-backed stablecoin, directly from and to fiat.
- **Purchase USDC** — on the networks Universel supports (detailed later in these docs).
- **Off-ramp** back to fiat when you're ready to cash out.
- **Facilitated transfers** — Universel facilitates transfers between parties, though the actual transfer is not executed through the service itself.
